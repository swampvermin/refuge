Refuge: A Collection of Urban Sanctuaries is a zine series exploring the historical evolution of urban green spaces in New York City, with a focus on their societal impact and modern cultural significance. The project covers Washington Square Park, Collect Pond Park, and the Brooklyn Heights Promenade. All zines and their contents were designed by Araceli Gonzalez-Rocha, as a part of her undergraduate thesis project. The zines were printed using a risograph printer, and you can learn more about the process here.
